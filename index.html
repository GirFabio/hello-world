About

Hi, I am PhD Candidate in Economics & Finance at Bocconi University. My research interests lie in the intersection between decision theory and financial economics. I focus on how risk and ambiguity affect the optimal decisions of individual agents and, at the aggregate level, how financial markets compensate investors to bear different types of uncertainty.
